Common Questions

Will oil stains come out?

Fresh oil usually comes out well. Older stains that have soaked deep into the concrete lighten a lot but can leave a shadow. We will tell you honestly what to expect when we see it.

How long does it take?

Most driveways take one to two hours. Bigger approaches take longer, and we confirm timing with your estimate.

Is high pressure safe for concrete?

Yes. Concrete is one of the few surfaces built for it. We use a surface cleaner rather than a bare wand, which cleans evenly and avoids the stripe marks you see in do-it-yourself jobs.
